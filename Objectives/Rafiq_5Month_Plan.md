# Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Insufficient interview access | Medium | High | Build contact list early; use warm intros; offer value exchange |
| Beachhead market unclear | Medium | High | Use scoring matrix; validate with 10+ segment interviews |
| MVP delays | High | Medium | Keep scope minimal; focus on core coaching flow only |
| Low user engagement | Medium | High | Recruit committed pilot users; provide incentives |
| Pricing uncertainty | Medium | Medium | Test multiple price points; use Van Westendorp method |
| Legal complexity | Low | Medium | Start with simple structures; defer complex items |
