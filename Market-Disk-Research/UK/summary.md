# United Kingdom Market Summary - Rafiq AI

## Quick Facts

**Market Size:**
- Total Teachers: 614,391 (513,436 in England)
- State Schools: ~24,900 schools
- Independent Schools: 2,642 schools
- Multi-Academy Trusts (MATs): 1,374 trusts managing 10,000+ schools
- Average MAT Size: 8 schools per trust

**Key Market Characteristics:**
- Mature EdTech ecosystem (#3 globally with 5,413 EdTech companies)
- Strong CPD and coaching culture
- Well-established MAT purchasing model
- Government target: All schools in MATs by 2030
- Annual EdTech market: $8.1B (2024), projected $46.4B by 2033

**Teacher Economics:**
- Starting Salary: £32,916 (£40,317 in London)
- Experienced (5 years): £45,352+
- Leadership: £51,771 - £153,488
- Average Salary: £31,550

**Competitive Landscape:**
- Mature market with established players
- IRIS Connect: 100,000+ teachers globally (dominant UK player)
- Century Tech: AI-powered personalized learning
- Strong lesson study and CPD culture

## Key Insights

### Strategic Opportunities

1. **Teacher Retention Crisis is Acute**
   - UK has 3rd worst teacher retention in OECD
   - 90% of leavers quit before retirement (vs 50% in other countries)
   - Government desperate for solutions: £233M in incentives
   - Workload reduction is #1 priority

2. **MAT Consolidation Advantage**
   - 1,374 MATs managing 10,000+ schools
   - Government target: All schools in MATs by 2030
   - Average 8 schools per MAT (larger MATs have 10-92 schools)
   - Centralized procurement enables enterprise deals

3. **Coaching Culture Exists BUT Underutilized**
   - Strong evidence base for instructional coaching
   - Paradox: "Most impactful, least used" CPD format
   - IRIS Connect proves market exists (100K teachers)
   - Opportunity: Make coaching scalable with AI

4. **London as Global EdTech Hub**
   - #1 in Europe for EdTech funding ($565M in 2024)
   - BETT Show provides global visibility
   - Easy access to VCs, accelerators, talent
   - Government actively supporting EdTech

### Strategic Challenges

1. **IRIS Connect Competition**
   - Founded 2007, cloud-based since 2011
   - 100,000+ teachers globally
   - Video-based collaborative learning platform
   - Adding AI insights (2025)
   - Must differentiate: AI-first vs video-first approach

2. **Data Protection Complexity**
   - Data Use and Access Act 2025 (new)
   - Only 1.6% of orgs ready for new law
   - Children's Code compliance
   - Schools are risk-averse with student data
   - Need robust data protection from day one

3. **School Budget Constraints**
   - EdTech budgets lower than 2019
   - 60% of startups struggle with pricing
   - Must prove clear ROI
   - Consider per-teacher vs per-school pricing

### Differentiation Strategy

**Position as:**
- AI-first (vs IRIS Connect's video-first approach)
- Workload reduction tool (save 6+ hours/week like Century)
- Early Career Framework aligned
- MAT-ready with centralized analytics
- Affordable alternative with AI scalability

**Key Message:**
"IRIS Connect for the AI age - reduce teacher workload while improving instruction through AI-powered coaching at scale"
