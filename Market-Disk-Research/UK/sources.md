## Teacher Retention Crisis Data

### OECD Data
- UK has worst teacher retention in OECD
- ~10% of English teachers leave profession annually
- Over 3x rates of France and Ireland
- 90%+ of UK teacher leavers quit before retirement

### Five-Year Attrition
- 1 year post-qualification: 89.7% still teaching
- 3 years post-qualification: 73.3% still teaching
- 5 years post-qualification: 67.7% still teaching
- Over 1/3 leave within 5 years

### Workload Data
- 90% of teachers considering leaving cite high workload
- Full-time teachers: 50-59 hour weeks
- Only 60% expect to remain for next 3 years (down from 75% pre-pandemic)

### CPD Quality Data (Teacher Development Trust 2025)
- 1 in 4 teachers received <1 day formal CPD in 2024/25
- 40% say CPD didn't clearly improve ability to perform role
- Coaching = "most impactful but least used" CPD format
