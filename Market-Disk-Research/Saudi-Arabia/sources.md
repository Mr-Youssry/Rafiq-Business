# Saudi Arabia - Research Sources

## Government & Official Sources

### Saudi Government
- **Ministry of Education**: www.moe.gov.sa/en
  - Education budget allocations (SAR 201B for 2025)
  - School statistics (26,248 public, 4,377 private schools)
  - AI curriculum rollout plans
  - Teacher transformation initiatives

- **Ministry of Investment (MISA)**: www.misa.gov.sa
  - Foreign investment licensing requirements
  - Capital requirements (SAR 500K minimum for LLC)
  - 2025 regulatory updates

- **SDAIA (Saudi Data & AI Authority)**: www.sdaia.gov.sa
  - Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) - Royal Decree M/19
  - AI Ethics Principles
  - Generative AI Guidelines
  - AI Adoption Framework (2025)
  - National Data Governance Platform

- **ETEC (National Authority for Education Evaluation)**
  - Teacher Standards and Professional Pathways
  - Teacher licensing framework

- **National eLearning Center**
  - Digital learning initiatives
  - Oracle partnership (50K AI training)

- **Vision 2030**: vision2030.gov.sa
  - Human Capability Development Program (HCDP)
  - Education transformation goals
  - Private sector participation targets

### Government Platforms
- **Madrasati Platform**: 98% student reach, UNESCO recognized
- **iEN (National Education Portal)**: Curriculum delivery, teacher tools
- **Tatweer Educational Technologies**: Developer of Madrasati
