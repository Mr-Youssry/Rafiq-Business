# Saudi Arabia - Ecosystem & Network

## Overview

Saudi Arabia has developed a mature, well-resourced startup ecosystem with substantial venture capital (STV $1.4B+), active accelerators (MISK, Flat6Labs, Badir), 200+ angel investors (OQAL), extensive school networks (Maarif, 26K+ public schools), and regular EdTech events (LEARN, EDGEx). The ecosystem is government-backed (Vision 2030), internationally connected (Google, Oracle partnerships), and rapidly expanding ($750M VC deployed in 2024).

**Score: 4.5/5** - One of the strongest startup ecosystems in MENA.
