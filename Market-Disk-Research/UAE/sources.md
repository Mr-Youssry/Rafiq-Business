# UAE - Research Sources

## Government & Official Sources

### UAE Federal Government
- **Ministry of Education**
  - Academic calendar 2024-2025
  - Digital Transformation in Education initiative
  - Minimum school days requirements (182 days)

- **Federal Budget 2024**
  - AED 10.2 billion education allocation
  - 15% of federal expenditures

### Dubai Government
- **Dubai Education Strategy 2033 (E33)**
  - Launched October 2024 by Sheikh Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum
  - Five strategic goals and 28 'Game Changer' initiatives
  - Target: 90% parent satisfaction, 100+ new schools by 2033

- **Dubai Plan 2033 / Dubai Economic Agenda D33**
  - Education as pillar of economic strategy

- **KHDA (Knowledge and Human Development Authority)**: khda.gov.ae
  - School inspection and rating system
  - Private school licensing
  - Marketing and advertising approval requirements

### Abu Dhabi Government
- **ADEK (Abu Dhabi Department of Education and Knowledge)**: adek.gov.ae
  - Private school regulation
  - Alef Education contract through 2033

- **Hub71**: hub71.com
  - Mubadala-backed tech ecosystem
  - 243 startups, $2.18B raised in 2024
  - $250M investment fund
  - Cohort 15 performance data

### National Strategies
- **UAE National AI Strategy 2031**
  - Cabinet adoption April 2019
  - Education as priority sector
  - AI governance framework

- **UAE Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)**
  - Federal Decree Law No. 45 of 2021
  - GDPR-aligned data protection requirements

## Legal & Regulatory Sources

### Company Formation
- **Freezone Setup Costs**: AED 5,000-30,000
- **IFZA Package**: ~AED 23,150 (1 visa)
- **DMCC Minimum Capital**: AED 50,000 per company

### Data Protection
- **UAE PDPL**: Federal Decree Law No. 45 of 2021
- **DIFC Data Law**: DIFC Law No. 5 of 2020
- Executive regulations pending (as of January 2025)

### AI Governance
- **Dubai AI Seal**: Voluntary certification by Dubai Centre for AI
- **Artificial Intelligence Office**: ai.gov.ae

### Tax Environment
- Corporate Tax: 9% (above AED 375,000), freezone exemptions available
- Personal Income Tax: 0%
- VAT: 5% (education services often exempt)
