- **MERSIS**: Central Trade Registry System
- **Turkish Commercial Code**: LLC and JSC requirements
- **Minimum Capital**: TRY 50,000 for LLC, TRY 250,000 for JSC
- **Sources**:

### OECD TALIS 2024
- **Website**: oecd.org/en/about/programmes/talis.html
- **Türkiye Country Profile**: gpseducation.oecd.org/CountryProfile?primaryCountry=TUR
- **Key Findings for Türkiye**:
  - Average working hours: 31.1/week (rank 51/55)
  - Average teacher age: 38 years (youngest in OECD)
  - Teachers 50+: 7.9% (lowest in OECD)
  - Contract satisfaction: 50%
  - New teacher induction: 56.5% (among lowest)
  - Class time on management: 21.5% (among highest)
