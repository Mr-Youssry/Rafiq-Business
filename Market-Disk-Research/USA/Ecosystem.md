### Verdict

**EXCEPTIONAL ECOSYSTEM:** The USA offers the world's most developed and supportive EdTech ecosystem. The combination of specialized VCs (with AI focus), established accelerators, major conferences (ASU+GSV, ISTE, FETC), credible media (EdSurge, TechCrunch), and district networks (CoSN) creates an unparalleled support system for EdTech startups. For Rafiq AI, this means clear pathways to funding, customers, partnerships, and credibility - critical advantages that don't exist in most other markets.
