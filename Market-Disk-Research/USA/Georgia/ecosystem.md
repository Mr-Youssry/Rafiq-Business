## Ecosystem Score: 4/5

### Strengths

- Strong accelerator/incubator ecosystem (CREATE-X, ATDC)
- Major tech hub (Atlanta) with VC access
- Clear pathway to national EdTech VCs
- GaETC provides efficient market access
- PAGE (100,000+ members) for teacher reach
- 16 RESAs as distribution partners
- State investment in coaching (aligned)

### Challenges

- No Atlanta-based EdTech-specific VC
- Limited EdTech angel community
- Must travel for major EdTech investor access
- Building network from scratch

### Key Ecosystem Actions

1. **Immediate:** Apply to CREATE-X or ATDC
2. **Q1 2025:** Attend GaETC (November 2025)
3. **Q1 2025:** Initiate RESA partnership conversations
4. **Q2 2025:** Attend ASU+GSV for national VC exposure
5. **Ongoing:** Build PAGE/GAE relationships
