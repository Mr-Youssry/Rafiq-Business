## 4.2 Pricing Strategy

### Recommended Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Target | Price (EGP/month) | Price (USD) | Features |
|------|--------|-------------------|-------------|----------|
| Free | Individual teachers | 0 | $0 | Basic reflection tools |
| Starter | Teachers (B2C) | 99-149 | $2-3 | Core coaching features |
| School | Small private schools | 299-499/teacher | $6-10 | Analytics, admin dashboard |
| Premium | International schools | 799-1,499/teacher | $16-30 | Full features, support |
| Enterprise | Chains, Ministry | Custom | Custom | Custom implementation |

### Pricing Model Comparison

| Model | Pros | Cons | Fit for Egypt |
|-------|------|------|---------------|
| Per Teacher | Simple, scalable | Usage may vary | Good for B2B |
| Per School (flat) | Predictable | Limits upsell | OK for small schools |
| Freemium | User acquisition | Conversion challenge | Good for awareness |
| Tiered Subscription | Flexibility | Complexity | Best overall |

### Price Differentiation by Segment

| Segment | Pricing Approach |
|---------|------------------|
| Public School Teachers | Freemium (acquisition) |
| Private School Teachers | Low-cost B2C |
| Private Schools (B2B) | Per-teacher licensing |
| International Schools | Premium pricing |
| Ministry/Government | Tender/custom |

## 4.4 Break-Even Analysis

### Assumptions
- Average revenue per user: EGP 200/month ($4)
- Variable cost per user: $2/month
- Fixed costs: EGP 30,000/month ($600)
- Gross margin: ~50%

### Break-Even Calculation

| Scenario | Users Needed | Notes |
|----------|--------------|-------|
| Minimal Fixed Costs | 150-200 | Bootstrap mode |
| Standard Operations | 300-400 | With 1-2 team members |
| Growth Mode | 500-750 | With marketing spend |

## 4.6 Retention Strategy

### Engagement Features
1. **Weekly coaching prompts** - AI-generated reflection questions
2. **Progress tracking** - Visual improvement metrics
3. **Peer communities** - Connect with other teachers
4. **Resource library** - Teaching strategies, templates
5. **Achievement badges** - Gamification elements

### Retention Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Definition |
|--------|--------|------------|
| D7 Retention | 50%+ | Active after 7 days |
| D30 Retention | 30%+ | Active after 30 days |
| Monthly Active Rate | 60%+ | % of subscribers active |
| Churn Rate | <5%/month | B2B target |
| NPS | 40+ | Net Promoter Score |

### Referral Ideas
1. **Teacher referral bonus** - Free months for referrals
2. **School referral** - Discount for school-wide adoption
3. **Case study incentives** - Feature successful users
4. **Ambassador program** - Lead teachers as advocates

### Customer Success Strategies
- Onboarding sequence (7-day email drip)
- In-app tutorials and tooltips
- Monthly check-in emails
- Quarterly user webinars
- Arabic support channels (WhatsApp)

## Viability Summary

### Revenue Potential
- **TAM (Egypt teachers):** 1M teachers x EGP 150/month = EGP 150M/month potential
- **SAM (Private + reachable):** ~150K teachers x EGP 200/month = EGP 30M/month
- **SOM (Year 1-2 realistic):** 500-2,000 users x EGP 200 = EGP 100-400K/month

### Profitability Path
1. **Break-even:** 300-400 paying users
2. **Sustainable:** 1,000+ users with low churn
3. **Growth mode:** 5,000+ users, expand to MENA
