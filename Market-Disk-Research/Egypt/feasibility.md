| Channel | Effectiveness | Cost |

### Operational Requirements

**Immediate (Pre-seed):**
- Founder-led sales (remote)
- Arabic localization (essential)
- Local payment processing (Fawry, instapay)
- Egyptian company registration (for credibility)

**Future (Seed/Growth):**
| Requirement | Timeline | Cost Estimate |
|-------------|----------|---------------|
| Local sales rep | 6-12 months | EGP 15K-25K/month |

### Localization Requirements
- **Language:** Arabic (Egyptian dialect) - Essential
- **Content:** Aligned with Egyptian curriculum context
- **Payment:** Local methods (Fawry, instapay, bank transfer)
- **Support:** Arabic customer support
- **Culturally appropriate:** Messaging, examples, case studies

## Feasibility Summary

### Execution Advantages
1. **Egyptian founder** - Cultural fit, language, local presence
2. **Growing VC ecosystem** - 37% CAGR, active EdTech investors
3. **Government support** - Multiple grant programs available
4. **Low setup costs** - ~$15-26K for year 1

### Execution Challenges
1. **No existing network** - Must build from scratch
2. **Long sales cycles** - 3-12 months for institutional
3. **Limited exit paths** - Fewer acquirers in EdTech
4. **Localization required** - Arabic essential
