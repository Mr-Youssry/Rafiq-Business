## Research Methodology Notes

### Data Limitations
- Teacher statistics latest comprehensive data from 2021/22
- Primary teacher data last updated 2016 (World Bank/UNESCO)
- PD budget data not systematically tracked
- Coaching adoption data limited to isolated research studies

### Recommended Primary Research
1. Direct surveys of Egyptian teachers (n=100+)
2. Interviews with school leaders (n=10+)
3. Ministry/GAFI consultations
4. EdTech founder interviews
5. Price sensitivity testing with target users
