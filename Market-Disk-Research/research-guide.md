## Research Framework

Each market should be evaluated across three dimensions aligned with our validation approach:

1. **Desirability** - Is there demand for this service?
2. **Feasibility** - Can we execute in this market?
3. **Viability** - Can we generate sustainable profit?

---

## Section 1: Legal & Regulatory Environment

### 1.1 General Legal Framework
- [ ] What are the general business regulations for foreign companies?
- [ ] What are the requirements for establishing a business entity?
- [ ] What are the data protection and privacy laws?

### 1.2 AI-Specific Regulations
- [ ] What are the current AI regulations in this country?
- [ ] Are there pending AI laws or frameworks being developed?
- [ ] Are there restrictions on AI use in education?

### 1.3 Education Sector Regulations
- [ ] What approvals are needed to sell to schools?
- [ ] Are there procurement requirements for educational technology?
- [ ] Are there certification or accreditation requirements for EdTech products?

## Section 2: Desirability - Market Demand

### 2.1 Market Structure & Size

#### Teacher Population
- [ ] What is the total number of teachers in this market?
- [ ] How is teacher population growing annually?
- [ ] What is the breakdown by education level (K-12, higher ed)?

#### Market Segmentation
- [ ] What is the market structure? (Public vs Private schools)
- [ ] What are the budget levels for public vs private institutions?
- [ ] Who are the actual customers? (Schools, districts, ministries, individual teachers)

### 2.2 Coaching Adoption

#### Current State of Coaching
- [ ] Is this market adopting instructional coaching?
- [ ] What is the structure of instructional coaching programs within schools?
- [ ] What is the structure of instructional coaching programs within districts/regions?
- [ ] How mature is the coaching culture in education?

#### Teacher Autonomy
- [ ] Is there flexibility for teachers to engage in instructional coaching independently?
- [ ] What features could we produce specifically for individual teachers?
- [ ] Do teachers have professional development budgets they control?

### 2.3 Investment in Professional Development

#### General PD Investment
- [ ] Do schools invest in professional development in general?
- [ ] In which areas do schools primarily invest for PD?
- [ ] What percentage of school budgets goes to PD?

#### Coaching-Specific Investment
- [ ] Do schools invest specifically in instructional coaching?
- [ ] What is the average budget for one teacher's instructional coaching per year?
- [ ] What is the minimum budget for teacher coaching per year?
- [ ] What is the maximum budget for teacher coaching per year?

### 2.4 Budget & Purchasing Cycles

#### Decision Making
- [ ] Who makes the budget decision for instructional coaching?
- [ ] Who are the key stakeholders in purchasing decisions?
- [ ] What is the typical approval process?

#### Timing
- [ ] What are the timeframes of budget allocation in this market?
- [ ] When does the academic year start/end?
- [ ] When are purchasing decisions typically made?
- [ ] What is the typical sales cycle length?

### 2.5 Competitive Landscape

#### Current Solutions
- [ ] What are the main alternatives or solutions customers currently use?
- [ ] How do customers currently solve the instructional coaching problem?
- [ ] Are there apps for instructional coaching already in this market?

#### Competitor Analysis
- [ ] Who are the direct competitors in this market?
- [ ] What are their pricing models?
- [ ] What are their strengths and weaknesses?

## Section 3: Feasibility - Can We Execute?

### 3.1 Network & Relationships

#### Existing Connections
- [ ] Do we have a network in this market?
- [ ] Do we have a contact in this country?
- [ ] Do we have warm introductions to gatekeepers in this market?

#### Early Adopter Potential
- [ ] Do we know a potential early adopter in this market?
- [ ] Have we identified innovation-friendly schools or districts?
- [ ] Are there EdTech champions we could connect with?

### 3.2 Sales & Operations

#### Sales Model
- [ ] Do we need local sales presence in this market?
- [ ] Can founders manage sales outreach remotely?
- [ ] What is the most effective outreach method to reach customers?

#### Operational Requirements
- [ ] Do we need future staff/partners in this market?
- [ ] Are there local partnership opportunities?
- [ ] What language/localization is required?

### 3.3 Funding & Investment Landscape

#### Market Investment Activity
- [ ] What is the amount invested in EdTech in this market annually?
- [ ] Does the market see M&A (Merging and Acquisition) deals in EdTech?
- [ ] What is the typical investment size for pre-seed/seed in EdTech?

#### Investor Access
- [ ] Can we access investors in this market?
- [ ] Do we know investors in this market?
- [ ] Are there active EdTech-focused VCs or angels?
- [ ] Are there government grants or programs for EdTech startups?

### 3.4 Resource Requirements
- [ ] What is the budget we need to fund our pre-seed first year in this market?
- [ ] What are the market entry costs specific to this country?
- [ ] What ongoing operational costs are specific to this market?

---

## Section 4: Viability - Profit Potential

### 4.1 Unit Economics

#### Pricing Research
- [ ] How much are customers willing to pay in this market?
- [ ] What is the cost of our service per user?
- [ ] What are the fixed costs of our service?
- [ ] What are the variable costs of our service?
- [ ] What are the costs specific to this market?
- [ ] What is the minimum viable price of our service?

#### Pricing Strategy
- [ ] Does our pricing change based on the market?
- [ ] Does our pricing change based on the type of institution?
- [ ] What pricing models work best? (per teacher, per school, subscription, etc.)

### 4.2 Revenue Model

#### Revenue Streams
- [ ] What are the different ways to generate revenue in this market?
- [ ] Are there additional revenue opportunities (training, consulting, data)?
- [ ] What is the potential for upselling/cross-selling?

### 4.3 Break-Even Analysis
- [ ] How many customers do we need to break even in this market?
- [ ] What is the realistic customer acquisition timeline?
- [ ] What is the expected customer lifetime value (LTV)?
- [ ] What is the expected customer acquisition cost (CAC)?

### 4.4 Market Validation Requirements

#### Customer Discovery
- [ ] How many customers could we interview to validate our assumptions?
- [ ] What is the profile of customers we need to interview?
- [ ] Is the initial segment large enough to deliver meaningful early traction?

#### Validation Metrics
- [ ] What response rate should we expect from outreach?
- [ ] How do we measure demand validation in this market?

### 4.5 Retention Strategy
- [ ] How do we keep customers engaged in the App?
- [ ] What simple retention or referral ideas could encourage repeat engagement?
- [ ] What are the key retention metrics to track? (repeat usage, renewal rates, referrals)
- [ ] How will we refine customer success strategies based on feedback?

## Section 5: Strategic Validation Questions

These questions help validate core assumptions and inform the overall business strategy.

### 5.1 Assumption Testing
- [ ] What are the key assumptions behind our idea in this market?
- [ ] What assumptions about customers need validation?
- [ ] What assumptions about the problem need validation?
- [ ] What assumptions about our revenue model need validation?

### 5.2 Customer Understanding
- [ ] Have we clearly defined who potential customers are?
- [ ] Why does this problem matter to them specifically in this market?
- [ ] What examples or data show problem frequency and cost (time, money, frustration)?

### 5.3 Value Proposition
- [ ] How should we define and estimate the Customer Value Proposition for this market?
- [ ] What points of differentiation are most relevant here?
- [ ] How does our solution perform vs. current alternatives?

### 5.4 Business Model Validation
- [ ] Which business model ideas seem most viable for this market?
- [ ] What aspects of the business model need further testing?
- [ ] Have we updated market size estimates based on outreach learnings?

### 5.5 Vision & Positioning
- [ ] How should we position Rafiq AI in this market?
- [ ] Is our vision statement relevant and motivating here?
- [ ] What messaging resonates with customers in this market?

### 5.6 Outreach Effectiveness
- [ ] What 1-2 simple outreach methods should we test first?
- [ ] What channels are most effective for reaching educators?
- [ ] What is the expected conversion from outreach to conversation?

### 5.7 Willingness to Pay Validation
- [ ] What drives users' willingness to pay in this market?
- [ ] How should we refine value proposition based on WTP insights?
- [ ] What pricing strategy adjustments are needed?
